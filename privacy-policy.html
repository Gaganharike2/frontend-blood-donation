Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 2026

Introduction

LifeLink respects the privacy of blood donors, hospitals, and platform users. This policy explains how data is collected, used, protected, and managed.

Data Collection

  • Personal identification information
  • Blood group and donation history
  • Location data for donor matching
  • Device and usage analytics
  • Hospital collaboration data

How We Use Data

  • Connecting donors with hospitals
  • Managing emergency blood requests
  • Sending alerts and notifications
  • Improving system performance
  • Enhancing platform security

Security & Protection

LifeLink uses encryption, secure authentication, and monitoring systems to protect all stored information and prevent unauthorized access.

User Rights

  • Access personal data
  • Update personal information
  • Request deletion of personal data
  • Withdraw consent
  • Export personal data

Regulatory Compliance

LifeLink complies with healthcare data protection standards including:

  • GDPR Privacy Regulations
  • HIPAA Healthcare Security Guidelines
  • National healthcare data protection laws
